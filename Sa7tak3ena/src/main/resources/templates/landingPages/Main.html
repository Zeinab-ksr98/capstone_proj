Our Services

Healthcare Accessibility

Improving Access to Care

We are reshaping healthcare accessibility in Lebanon by providing real-time updates on ICU and NICU bed availability. We ensure all contact information is consolidated in one place, and we help allocate the nearest available hospital and ambulance to those in need. Our platform connects hospitals, patients, and ambulance services efficiently.

Hospital Management System

Our Hospital Management System simplifies the process of managing reservations and requests. Hospitals can efficiently allocate resources by identifying the nearest available hospital for incoming patients. With our system, hospitals can streamline their operations and improve patient care.

Ambulance Management System

Our Ambulance Management System provides a seamless way to manage ambulance vehicles and requests. Dispatchers can easily assign and track ambulances, ensuring timely responses to emergencies. With real-time monitoring, dispatchers can efficiently allocate resources and improve emergency response times.
